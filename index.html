One thing that too many of us who stutter share is silence. Stuttering was something I'd never talked about, and my inability to talk about it left me wondering about a lot of things. What is stuttering? Where does it come from? Is it passed down through families, or was it just in mine?

The Way We Talk is about exploring a part of me I'd always wished wasn't there, and the role it's played in my identity and my family story.
